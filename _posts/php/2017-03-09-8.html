1.Password： The password must be a minimum of 8 characters having at least 1 number, 1 lower, 1 upper, and 1 special character.

pattern='/^(?=.*[0-9])(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*[@$%^&+=])(?=\\S+$).{8,}$/'

pattern='/^(?=.*[0-9])(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*[@$%^&+=!\"'()*,-./:;<>?\\[\\\\\\]_`{|}~])(?=\\S+$).{8,}$/'

2.将string中的非数字去掉

string.replace(/[^0-9]/g, "")
